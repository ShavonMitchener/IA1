My Personality Traits & Their Impact

  • Determined: My determination helps me stay committed to my goals, especially when schoolwork becomes challenging. It pushes me to complete assignments on time and study consistently, even when I feel overwhelmed.

  • Creative: Creativity allows me to approach academic tasks from different angles. Whether it’s solving technical problems or preparing presentations, I use original ideas to make my work stand out and find unique solutions.

  • Adaptive: Being adaptive helps me adjust quickly to new subjects, teaching styles, or group projects. It makes it easier to work with different classmates and stay calm when unexpected changes happen in school.

My Hobbies & Their Impact

  • Spending Time with Friends: Socializing with friends helps me maintain emotional balance and reduce stress, which keeps me motivated and focused during challenging school assignments and exams.

  • Playing Video Games: Gaming improves my quick thinking, decision-making, and concentration. These skills help me stay alert in class and solve academic problems more effectively, especially in technical subjects.

  • Reading Manga: Reading manga boosts my creativity and imagination. It also gives me mental breaks that prevent burnout, allowing me to return to studying with a fresh and relaxed mindset.

Some of My Favorite Video Games

Stellar Blade

Stellar Blade is an action-adventure game where players take on the role of Eve, a soldier from an orbital colony, tasked with reclaiming Earth from the Naytibas, monstrous aliens. The game emphasizes fast-paced, third-person combat that integrates parrying and dodging, while also allowing for exploration of a post-apocalyptic Earth. Eve's mission involves fighting to save humanity's remnants and uncovering the complex backstory behind the world's devastation.

Black Myth Wukong

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG where players control a monkey-like figure known as the "Destined One" on a quest inspired by the classic Chinese novel Journey to the West. The game's story involves the Destined One, who is a version of Sun Wukong, seeking to recover six relics scattered after a great battle to either reclaim or restore the legend of the Monkey King. Gameplay focuses on challenging combat, combining various abilities, and exploring a world filled with Chinese mythology.




Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a story-rich, open-world action RPG where you play as the "Rover," who wakes up with amnesia and journeys across the world of Solaris-3 to uncover their past and origins. Along the way, they meet and team up with companions called Resonators, fight enemies, and get caught up in a larger conflict involving a cataclysm called the "Lament" and its resulting monsters known as Tacet Discords. The combat features combo-heavy action, high mobility, and monster taming, and the story unfolds through main quests that players unlock as they level up.

My Hero

:

Christian Bale

"If you have a problem with me text me, if you don't have my number you don't know me well enough to have a problem with me"
— Christian Bale

Background Information

Christian Bale, born on January 30, 1974, in Pembrokeshire, Wales, began his career as a child and became a highly versatile actor. His family has a strong performing arts background, with a mother who was a dancer and a clown, an actress sister, and a grandfather who worked as a stunt double for John Wayne in the 1962 film Hatari!

Inspiration

I chose Christian Bale as my hero because his quote, “If you have a problem with me text me, if you don’t have my number you don’t know me well enough to have a problem with me,” really resonates with me. It reminds me not to waste energy worrying about what distant people think, and instead to focus on those who truly know and understand me. If someone is close enough to have my number, it means they’re close enough to talk things through honestly. Anyone outside of that circle doesn’t need to have an opinion that affects my peace of mind.

AI Business Idea Review

Chosen Idea: AI-Powered Virtual Assistants

Everyone uses a virtual assistant, be it asking Siri to set an alarm or using a personal finance app to create expense reports. Virtual assistants and agents are great AI-based business ideas for startups to build products of exponential value.

  • Personal Assistants: Automating workflows like sending emails, paying bills, ordering groceries, or booking appointments.
  • Customer Support Assistants: AI chatbots acting as service agents to help users find information and solve issues.
  • Expert Assistants: Specialized AI in stock trading, weather, travel booking, or research.
  • Executive Assistants: AI that helps leaders schedule meetings, summarize documents, and plan follow-ups.

Application in My World

As a Computer Network Security student, a virtual assistant could help me organize my study schedule, automate reminders for assignments, and even summarize long technical readings into manageable notes. This would improve my time management and academic productivity.

Ethical Concerns

The main concerns with virtual assistants are data privacy and over-dependence on AI. Since these assistants collect sensitive personal information, there is always the risk of misuse, hacking, or data leaks.

My Solution

To address these issues, companies should implement strong encryption, transparent privacy policies, and give users full control over their data. Virtual assistants should act as a supplement to, not a replacement for, human judgment and decision-making.

Real-World Example

Two powerful examples of virtual assistants today are ChatGPT by OpenAI and Google Gemini. These AI assistants can hold conversations, answer questions, create content, and even help with productivity tasks like summarizing documents or generating ideas. They demonstrate how AI can provide practical support to individuals, students, and professionals alike.
